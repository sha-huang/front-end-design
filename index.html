Welcome to Little Lemon!
Great deals for 1st time visitors

We are a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. Inspired by Italian, Greek and Turkish culture, we rotate seasonally a menu of 12-15 items.

Menu for the Season

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian. To craft the menu, Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy. Adrian leads the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mediterranean region.
